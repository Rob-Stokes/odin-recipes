Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 6oz butter
  • 6oz granulated sugar
  • 6oz brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 12oz self-raising flour
  • 8oz chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 160 C (320 F) fan-forced.
  2. Cream butter with sugar.
  3. Beat in eggs.
  4. Fold in sifted flour.
  5. Add chocolate chips.
  6. Drop teaspoons of mixture onto tray with baking parchment.
  7. Bake for 12 mins.

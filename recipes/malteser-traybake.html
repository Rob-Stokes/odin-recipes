Malteser Traybake

Description

Malteser mixed with chocolate, crushed digestives, and more chocolate

Ingredients

  • 100g butter
  • 200g milk chocolate
  • 3 tablespoons golde syrup
  • 225g digestive biscuits
  • 225g Maltesers
  • 100g white chocolate

Method

  1. Melt together the butter, chocolate and syrup then add the crushed biscuits and the Maltesers
  2. Mix together quickly then pour into a lined tin and chill until set
  3. Drizzle with some melted white chocolate
